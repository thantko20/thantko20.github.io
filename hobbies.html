Football

Football (or Americans would say "Soccer") has been my favourite sports so far. You can say that I was born with football. It's never tiring to play football. Hot or rainy, whatever the conditions are, I'm always ready to play. Below are some of my favourite players:

Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi has won 5 ballon d'ors. He'd become my favourite player when I was in Grade-7. Most people praise him as the greatest of all time. Some even say he comes from a another planet!

Wayne Rooney

Wayne Rooney is Man Utd's all time highest goal scorer and legend. I've been his fan since I started to support Man Utd as a kid.

Marco Reus

You've probably guessed it. My nickname comes after Marco Reus. Admittedly though, I like him because of his good looks. He is a talented player but also injury prone.
